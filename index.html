— Frequently asked

Answers, plainly.

If your question isn't here, email hi@usepodpick.com. We answer every email within 24 hours.

Do you guarantee podcast bookings?+
No. We promise hand-written pitches sent on your behalf. Whether a host books you depends on your story, the host's calendar, audience fit, and timing — none of which we control. We've never met a credible PR agency that guarantees bookings, and you should be suspicious of any that does.
What does "sent from my inbox" actually mean?+
During intake, you connect your Gmail or Outlook account through a one-click OAuth flow. We send pitches through that connection. Hosts see emails from you, not from us. Replies route back to you. You stay in control of the relationship.
How long until my first pitches go out?+
Once intake is complete and your inbox is connected, first pitches go out within 5 business days. Most clients finish intake in a single sitting and see their first pitches sent before the trial week is over.
How is this different from podcast booking software?+
Self-serve software hands you a database, a template, and the responsibility to do the work yourself. We do the work: research, writing, sending, follow-up, reply forwarding. You spend zero hours per month on it. Software is for people who want to learn a new tool. Podpick is for people who just want the outcome.
What if I want to pause for a month?+
Pause anytime from your dashboard. Your subscription holds, pitch generation stops, you can resume whenever. Many clients pause during book launches or busy quarters and pick back up later. No penalty.
Can I cancel?+
Yes, anytime, from your dashboard. No phone calls, no retention specialists, no contracts. If you cancel mid-month you keep service through the end of the period you've already paid for.
What if I'm new to podcasts and don't know my angle yet?+
The intake form walks you through clarifying your angle. If you finish intake still uncertain, your VA can help you sharpen it over a short message thread before pitches start. We'd rather get the angle right than send three weeks of pitches that don't land.
Will I know which podcasts you're pitching?+
Yes. Your dashboard shows every podcast pitched with the host name, audience size, episode referenced, and the actual pitch we sent. Full visibility, no surprise sends.
